OUR STORY

From a single store in 2004 to Malaysia's leading pizza delivery company

WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2004, PizzaPals has grown from a single location in Machang, Kelantan, into the largest pizza company in Malaysia based on retail sales. With more than 7,000 stores in over 10 countries, PizzaPals is a company of exceptional people on a mission to be the best pizza delivery company in the world.

Our fundamental business is pizza delivery, carried out by a network of dedicated corporate and franchise stores. We continue to focus on our core strengths of order-taking, pizza-making, and door-to-door delivery, constantly improving each element to provide better customer service.

At PizzaPals, we don't just make pizza. We make people happy. That's our mission, and it's what drives us to get better at what we do. From our chefs creating new recipes to our technologists building easier ordering systems, we're all about making the pizza experience better from start to finish.
